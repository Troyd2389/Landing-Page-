Most People Are Guessing

Most people have no clear picture of their current health and fitness level.

They may know their weight.
They may know how they feel.
They may know they “should” exercise more.

But they rarely know which physical capabilities matter most, where they are strong, where they are falling behind, or what to do next.

Relentless replaces guesswork with awareness, direction, and a system for improvement.

A Score That Creates Awareness

Relentless is being built around a simple idea: when people understand where they stand, they are more likely to take action.

The Relentless Score is designed to help people see their current level, identify gaps, and understand what needs to improve — without getting lost in confusing data or random workouts.

Built With Technology

Why Relentless Exists

People deserve a better way to understand their health and fitness — and a better system for improving it.

Family / founder image coming soon

Relentless started from a simple belief: people deserve a better way to understand their health and fitness — and a better system for improving it.

For Troy, fitness began through basketball and the drive to jump higher, compete harder, and build confidence through training. Over time, that evolved into a deeper understanding of what fitness really provides: strength, resilience, mental clarity, and the ability to show up for the people who matter most.

Today, the mission is bigger than workouts.

Relentless is about helping people build a stronger body, a sharper mind, and better long-term capability — so they can keep playing, exploring, working, competing, parenting, and living fully for decades.
