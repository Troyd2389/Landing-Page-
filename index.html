The Relentless Mission

Relentless is a commitment to never stop improving, no matter what life throws at you.

It means using adversity, stress, and challenge as fuel to become stronger physically, mentally, and emotionally.

It means showing up when it is hard, pushing forward when others quit, and constantly working to build a stronger body, sharper mind, and better life.

It is not about perfection — it is about getting stronger every day.

A Better System for Health and Fitness

Most people are left guessing. They may know their weight, how they feel, or that they should exercise more — but they often do not know which physical capabilities matter most, where they are improving, or where they are falling behind.

Relentless is being built to replace guesswork with awareness, direction, and action.

Keep Going

Built From a Bigger Why

Relentless was created by Troy, a health and fitness coach, husband, and father who believes fitness is about far more than workouts.

For Troy, the mission is personal: to stay strong and capable for his family, to help others take ownership of their health, and to build a system that gives people a clearer path forward.

His wife Elizabeth, a registered nurse with experience in preventive and age-management health, has helped shape the belief that people need to be proactive, informed, and involved in their long-term well-being.

Relentless brings together coaching, objective assessment, AI-enabled tools, and real human guidance to help people become physically capable for life.
